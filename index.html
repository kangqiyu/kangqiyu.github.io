Biography - Oct, 2022

I am currently a research fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering in NTU. Currently, I focus on

  • applying differential geometry to Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and bringing physical insights to design and analyze new GNNs.
  • improving neural network robustness.
I received the B.S. degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2015, and the Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological Universty (NTU) in 2020. My Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Tay Wee Peng. My research interests include applied probability, differential geometry, and machine learning.

News - Oct, 2022

One paper accpeted in NeurIPS 2022

In this paper, named "On the Robustness of Graph Neural Diffusion to Topology Perturbations", we provide insights of the graph neural PDEs' robustness by exploiting the stability of the heat semigroup under graph topology perturbations. We discuss various graph diffusion operators and relate them to existing graph neural PDEs. Furthermore, we propose a general graph neural PDE framework based on which a new class of robust GNNs can be defined.
